EXPIRIENCE

VIRTUAL REALITY

IN HISTORIC ROUTES

“Digital Routes in Greek History” is an R&D project that allow users to have a lively experience through the steps of the Hellenic History, in a wide timeframe, from antiquity to modern times. Historical Periods and Facts are revived and connected with the Hellenic Scenery through travel routes and virtual tours of important and historical cities.

Therefore, the platform successfully  unfolds important historical periods of the Hellenic History in a Journey through time and location.

The user, with the help of an interactive timeline, is able to create personal routes through Hellenic History and learn about important events by visiting virtually the Hellenic cities that took place. All these by activating the application and use the virtual reality mask (cardboard).

IHR/NHRF

Institute of Historical Research

The Institute of Historical Research of the National Hellenic Research Foundation (IHR/NHRF) conducts research on the political, economic, social and cultural history of Greek and Roman Antiquity, Byzantium and modern Hellenism. The Institute emerged in 2012 from the merging of the three former Humanities research institutes of the NHRF (KERA, IBR and INR), the oldest research institutions for historical and philological sciences in Greece.

CULTECH

Cultural Technology Lab 

The Cultural Technology Lab is a new laboratory established in 2016 at the Dept. of Informatics & Telecommunications  of the National & Kapodistrian University of Athens. Its main target is the research and the evaluation of the emerging ICT technologies in the sector of Culture and Creative Industries.

COMIC

CONTENT MANAGEMENT IN CULTURE

 COMIC was founded in 2013 and has since been active in the field of IT and telecommunications services, specializing in the cultural sector. The company covers a wide range of services related to the development of projects and IT solutions in the area of cultural content, the implementation of digital applications and all related support services for the implementation of private and public projects.
