Undergraduate education

In the undergraduate program, special emphasis is placed on acquiring knowledge and skills in programming of all types (e.g. big data, artificial intelligence, software, hardware, internet of things, machine learning, signal and information processing, telecommunications, networks, space) and consists of two two-year study cycles: the basic study cycle that offers basic knowledge in the entire range of Information Technology and Telecommunications and the focused study cycle, where students initially choose one of two directions, either direction A (Computer Science) or direction B (Computer Engineering) and then they can register up to two specializations in which the Department has a very high-level research activity.

According to the decision of the Assembly of the Department of Informatics & Telecommunications and its approval by the Senate, the PDE certificate is granted exclusively to those students or graduates of the Department who are successfully evaluated in a specific group of courses with a total of 30 ECTS.
